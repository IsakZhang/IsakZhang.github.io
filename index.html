Wenxuan Zhang (Isaac)

wxzhang [at] se.cuhk.edu.hk

Hi, I am currently a third-year Ph.D. student at the Text Mining Group in The Chinese University of Hong Kong, where I am advised by Prof. Wai LAM to conduct research on natural language processing (NLP).

I have a broad interest in NLP and machine learning problems, especially utilizing deep learning for various interesting real-world NLP tasks. Currently, my major research interest focuses on low-resource NLP.

More about me: Google Scholar / Zhihu / Blog

Research Interests

  • Low-resource NLP (cross-lingual and cross-domain settings)
  • Question Answering (especially product-related QA in E-commerce)
  • Information Verification: Fact Checking etc.
  • Sentiment Analysis and Text Summarization
